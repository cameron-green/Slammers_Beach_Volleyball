Goals

The website is intended to increase customers for Slammers Beach Volleyball by promoting our services in order to attract new customers to sign up online and/or travel on site for these services.

Success Evaluation

Other advertising will be kept consistent for a period until the end of August (except for the new web site), so that any increase in customers will be attributable to the site.

At the end of August (and during the period from site launch), the number of new customers through the door and the number of new sign-ups will be counted and compared to the previous period.

Target Audience

  1. This site is aimed at a new customer base of local residents and families, between the ages of 15 - 30.

  2. This site will be designed for this new younger customer base by use of the following:

    • Use of lively colours
    • Scalable - Suitable for use on mobile phones
    • Emphasis on specials
    • Emphasis on Signing Up for competitions
    • Calls to action on each page
